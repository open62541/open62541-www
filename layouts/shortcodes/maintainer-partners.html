Official Maintainer

o6 Automation GmbH

o6 Automation GmbH is the official maintainer of open62541. They provide professional support, consulting, and custom development services for OPC UA implementations.

With deep expertise in industrial automation and real-time systems, o6 Automation ensures the continued development and stability of the open62541 project.

Partners

open62541 is supported by leading research institutions and industry organizations

Fraunhofer IOSB

The Fraunhofer Institute for Optronics, System Technologies and Image Exploitation IOSB was a core maintainer of open62541 for many years until the spin-off to o6 Automation. They continue to maintain a close connection to the project and provide research expertise and industrial application experience in automation and communication technologies.

OSADL

The Open Source Automation Development Lab (OSADL) is a network organization dedicated to promoting open source software in industrial automation and real-time applications. In the past, OSADL served as an organizer for funding of development activities for open62541, facilitating collaboration between industry partners, research institutions, and developers to advance the adoption of open source technologies in mission-critical automation systems.
